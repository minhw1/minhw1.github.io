What is korean wave?

korean wave refers to the phenomenon in which things related to Korea, including Korean pop culture, become popular outside of Korea.

Music

K-pop did a great job in promoting Korean culture. As a result, pop events are held overseas for overseas fans. Typical examples are kcon and mama. In particular, the idol culture that only Korea has surprised many people.

Drama

It is called K-drama.Currently, it has become a central culture that leads the Korean Wave along with K-POP and is exported to various countries.
Korea is also called the world's top three drama powerhouses. In fact, Korea (32 dramas) has exported a lot of dramas to the world after the United States (58 films) and the United Kingdom (40 films) for five years from 2013 to 2018.

Food

Korean food can be largely divided into main dishes, side dishes, and desserts.
The main dishes include rice, porridge, and noodles, and side dishes include stir-fried vegetables, grilled fish, etc.
Desserts are largely divided into rice cakes, snacks, and drinks.
